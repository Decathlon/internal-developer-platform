## Responsibilities

Maintainers are responsible for:

- Reviewing and merging pull requests
- Triage issues
- Managing releases
- Enforcing the Code of Conduct
- Setting the technical direction of the project

## Becoming a Maintainer

Active contributors who have demonstrated a commitment to the project may be invited to become maintainers.
We look for:

- Consistent and high-quality contributions
- Helpful and respectful interactions with other community members
- A deep understanding of the codebase and project goals

If you are interested in becoming a maintainer, please reach out to one of the current maintainers.
